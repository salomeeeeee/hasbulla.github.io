Who is Hasbulla Magomedov?

Hasbulla was born on 7th July 2002 and is 20 years old, although he has a babyface, childlike features and a high-pitched voice. Hasbulla Magomedov was born and grew up in Makhachkala, Dagestan. He took to social media during the height of the pandemic and began posting content to Instagram and TikTok where his notability grew dramatically. Early social media posts showed his hotheaded nature and the high jinks of his life. Later posts saw him sporting fighting gear, routinely boxing, striking & grounding opponents in the gym.
